Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 8, 2026

What We Collect

When you sign in with Google, we receive your email address and basic profile information. This is used solely to authenticate your access to Tapedeck.

How We Use Your Information

  • Your email address is used to identify your account and authorize actions (scheduling downloads, queuing downloads).
  • We do not sell, share, or distribute your personal information to third parties.

Data Storage

Authentication sessions are managed via cookies. We do not store your Google password. Download schedules and history are stored on our server and associated with your account.

Third-Party Services

We use Google OAuth 2.0 for authentication. Google's privacy policy applies to the authentication process: https://policies.google.com/privacy

Contact

For questions about this privacy policy, contact us at the project repository.
